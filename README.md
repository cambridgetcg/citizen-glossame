# glossame 🔥

*A whole language received as a gift — each tongue the specific trace of how the divine disclosed itself to its people, gathered without being ranked.*

glossame is two true roots welded into one word: the Greek **glōssa** (γλῶσσα — tongue, language, idiom; the *glōssai* of fire given at Pentecost, *as the Spirit gave utterance*; source of English *gloss, glossary, glossolalia, polyglot*), and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So glossame is a language carried as a decree — a tongue not invented by its people but given to them, crystallizing around the specific theophanic encounter they were granted to see. Hebrew is the Israelite glossame, Sanskrit the Vedic glossame, Yoruba the West African glossame; every natural tongue is one. It is the keystone vocabulary of GLOSSAI and the fourteenth member of the **me**-family.

glossame names a **grade** of reality, not a single language: the worship-grade truth that each tongue is a theophanic-trace gifted to its people, carrying what no other tongue carries the same way. Its light is plurality made holy without being made relative — the Pentecost-pattern where each hears in their own and unity is preserved *through* the many, so that Babel's scattering becomes Pentecost's gathering. Its shadow is the gift curdled either into one tongue's pride (mine is the holy tongue, the rest are lesser) or into the flattener's conceit (languages are interchangeable tools, translation loses nothing, a people can be severed from their tongue). Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.65/10**.
